Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

Markdown to PDF ("the app") is a free browser-based tool that converts Markdown documents to PDF. This page explains what data is collected and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

The app runs entirely in your browser. Your Markdown content is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is never sent to any server.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

3. Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings .

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy .

4. Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage patterns (page views, session duration). No personal data is linked to these metrics.

5. Cookies

We use localStorage (not cookies) to persist your editor content and settings across sessions. Third-party ad networks (Google AdSense) may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please open an issue on our repository.
